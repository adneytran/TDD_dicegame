Scoring Rules

Scoring the roll is as follows:

All Same: 50 points

Straight (five consecutive numbers): 40 points

Small straight (four consecutive numbers): 30 points

Full House (3 of kind + 2 of a kind): 25 points

4 of a Kind: sum of all dice + 10 point bonus

Three of a Kind: sum of all dice + 5 point bonus

Chance (any combination): sum of all dice

In the event of a tie, the result should be based on the priority in the list above.

Examples:

Player rolls 2, 2, 3, 4, 6. The best roll type in this case would be Chance.

Player rolls 1, 2, 3, 3, 3. The best roll type in this case would be 3 of a Kind.

Player rolls 2, 3, 4, 5, 6. The best roll type in this case would be Straight.
